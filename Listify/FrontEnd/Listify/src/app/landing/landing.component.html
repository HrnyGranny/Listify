Manage Your Shopping Lists Efficiently

Listify is shopping list app created to help you manage your shopping lists efficiently. With Listify, you can easily create, edit, and share your shopping lists with others. Our app is designed to make your shopping experience as smooth and convenient as possible.

Free Version

Users who have not paid can create up to 2 lists, each list can have a maximum of 10 products, and they can only share it with 1 person.

Premium Version

Users who have paid can create as many lists as they want, add all the products they desire, and share them with as many people as they want. The payment is made by card, costs 3 euros, and is valid for 1 year.
